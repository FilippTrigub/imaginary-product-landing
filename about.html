About NovaSphere

NovaSphere brings together engineers, designers and thinkers to build tools that extend human creativity. Our mission is to democratize immersive computing while prioritizing privacy, accessibility and sustainability.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, NovaSphere began as a small research project and quickly grew into a team focused on reimagining the personal computing experience. We combine novel hardware with adaptive software to create intuitive holographic experiences.
