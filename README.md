# NovaSphere · The Future of Personal Computing

NovaSphere reimagines how humans and machines collaborate. This landing experience introduces a concept device that floats luminous holograms above your desk, interprets gestures with sub-millisecond precision, and anticipates your next move through adaptive AI.

## Vision
- **Seamless Spatial Interaction** — Project data into mid-air, rearrange it with a wave, and collaborate around a shared 3D canvas.
- **Adaptive Intelligence** — NovaSphere learns your workflows, offering context-aware shortcuts and predictive insights.
- **Human-Centric Design** — Every animation, color, and sound cue is crafted to reduce friction and amplify focus.

## Tech Stack
- **HTML5** for semantic structure
- **CSS3** with custom gradients and glassmorphism accents
- **Vanilla JavaScript** for subtle motion and interaction queues

## Getting Started
1. Clone this repository.
2. Open `index.html` in your preferred browser.
