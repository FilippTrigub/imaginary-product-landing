Meet Our Team

The brilliant minds behind the future of computing

Leadership

Alex Chen

Chief Executive Officer

Former SpaceX engineer turned entrepreneur, Alex brings 15 years of experience in cutting-edge technology development. He holds a PhD in Quantum Computing from MIT and has led multiple successful tech startups.

Sarah Rodriguez

Chief Technology Officer

A visionary technologist with a passion for holographic interfaces, Sarah previously worked at Google DeepMind. She has published over 50 research papers on neural networks and human-computer interaction.

Engineering Team

Lead Hardware Engineer

David specializes in quantum processor architecture and has been instrumental in developing NovaSphere's revolutionary energy harvesting technology. He holds 12 patents in semiconductor design.

Emily Watson

Research & Development
