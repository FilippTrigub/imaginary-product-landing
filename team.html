Meet Our Team

The visionary minds architecting the future of human-computer interaction

Sarah Chen

CEO & Founder

A former quantum computing researcher at MIT with a Ph.D. in Applied Physics, Sarah pioneered breakthrough algorithms in quantum entanglement before founding NovaSphere. Her vision combines cutting-edge science with human-centered design, driving our mission to make transformative technology accessible to everyone. Under her leadership, NovaSphere has grown from a garage startup to an industry leader in immersive computing.

Marcus Rodriguez

Chief Technology Officer

With over 15 years pioneering holographic display systems and spatial computing interfaces, Marcus brings unparalleled technical expertise to NovaSphere. Previously leading advanced R&D at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, he developed the holographic visualization systems used in Mars rover missions. Marcus orchestrates our engineering teams, ensuring every line of code and hardware component meets the highest standards of innovation and reliability.

Dr. Aisha Patel

Head of Research & AI

Dr. Patel holds dual doctorates in Neuroscience and Computer Science from Stanford, where she pioneered adaptive neural networks that mimic human cognitive patterns. As our Head of Research, she leads a world-class team developing NovaSphere's intelligent systems—from predictive interfaces to neural security protocols. Her groundbreaking work in brain-computer interfaces has earned her recognition as one of MIT Technology Review's "35 Innovators Under 35."

James Wilson

Chief Design Officer

James spent a decade as Lead Designer at Apple, where he shaped the visual language of iOS and contributed to the design of the Apple Watch. His design philosophy—"technology should be felt, not seen"—permeates every aspect of NovaSphere's user experience. James leads our global design team in crafting interfaces that are not only visually stunning but intuitively natural, ensuring that powerful technology never gets in the way of human creativity.
