# Quantum Physics Summary

Quantum physics, or quantum mechanics, describes nature at the scale of atoms and subatomic particles. Core principles include wave-particle duality, quantization of energy, and the uncertainty principle, which limits how precisely position and momentum can be known simultaneously.
